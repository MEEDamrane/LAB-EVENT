Guidelines:

  • For Authors: At least one author of each accepted paper must be registered to ensure its inclusion in the conference proceedings and to schedule it for presentation, otherwise the papers will not be published.
  • Publication and Presentation Limit: For each registration, a maximum of one article will be published and presented. If authors wish to present more than one article, they must pay an additional fee for each extra article, with a limit of one additional article.
  • Individual Participation: One registration provides participation in the conference for only one person. If multiple authors wish to attend, each must register individually.

Submission Guidelines

The official language of all the conferences and papers is English. Therefore, the paper must be written in English, which must be straightforward and free of grammatical errors.

Call for Papers

ICRAEEE 2025 invites original contributions in the areas of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Renewable Energy Systems and Energy Storage, Environmental Technology and Sustainable Practices, and Materials and Applications.

The conference aims to bring together researchers, engineers, and practitioners to discuss recent advancements and innovations in these key fields.

Important Dates

  • Abstract Submission Deadline: July 30, 2025
  • Full Paper Submission Deadline: August 30, 2025
  • Notification of Acceptance: September 15, 2025
  • Publication of the Program: November 15, 2025
  • Conference Dates: December 11-12, 2025

Paper Submission Instructions

Abstract Submission

  • Authors must first submit an abstract of no more than 200 words.
  • The abstract should clearly outline the problem, methodology, results, and conclusions.
  • Authors will receive a notification regarding the abstract's acceptance within two weeks.
  • Abstracts must be sent by email to icraeee25@gmail.com, specifying the topic of their presentation.

Full Paper Submission

  • Upon abstract acceptance, authors must submit the full paper by August 30, 2025.
  • All papers and abstracts must be submitted through the official ICRAEEE 2025 submission platform.
  • Authors will need to create an account on the platform to submit their work.
  • Manuscripts should be at least 5 pages (including figures, tables, and references).

Review Process

All submitted papers will undergo peer review by experts in the respective fields. The conference proceedings will include only accepted papers.

Formatting and Style

  • Papers must be formatted according to the ICRAEEE conference template. The template is available on the conference website.
  • Download Abstract Template
  • Download Full Paper Template
  • Authors must ensure proper citation of previous works and avoid plagiarism.

Presentation Formats

  • Oral Presentations: Authors of selected papers will be invited to present their work in oral sessions during the conference.
  • Poster Presentations: Authors of papers that are not selected for oral presentation may be invited to present their work in poster format.
  • Presentation guidelines will be shared with authors upon acceptance.

Conference Journals

All accepted full papers will be published in the E2S Web of Conferences indexed in Scopus.

The papers selected from the accepted submissions will be published in one of the journals indexed in Scopus or Web of Science.
