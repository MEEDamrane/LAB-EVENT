Conference publication

ICRAEEE 2025 may collaborate with one or more partner journals to publish a special issue.

Only papers presented at the conference by one of the authors will be eligible for consideration in the special issue.
Manuscripts must be submitted following the instructions outlined in the invitation letter.Each submission will undergo a review process in accordance with the journal's guidelines.

Authors are required to update the conference on the progress of their submission using the method specified in the invitation letter.
